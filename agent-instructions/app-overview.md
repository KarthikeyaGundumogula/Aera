🎬 FrameHouse — Product Vision & System Blueprint

🌌 1. Introduction
FrameHouse is a digital theatre for cinematic expressions—a space where audiences evolve into Artists, and media becomes a canvas for expression.

🧠 Core Belief
Stories don’t end when the credits roll.They evolve through those who experience them.
FrameHouse exists to capture that evolution. Its mission is to create a space where fans can reinterpret media, build identity through meaning, and be recognized for their creativity—not just their content. Its a Living Cinema.

FrameHouse is:
A space where media is reinterpreted, and creators build identity through meaning—not metrics.

🏆 6. The Recognition System
🔹 Credits (Core Mechanism)
* Credits carry intent
* Credits are awarded to works of artists from others
* Credits can have weight
* Credits can define identity over time

Types of Credits
  1. Quick Credits
     * universal reactions (e.g., “wow”, “celebrate”)
     * low weight
     * expressive, not impactful

  2. Creator Credits
     * created by recognized artists
     * carry weight + identity
     * influence visibility and ranking
  
☀️ Presence (profile-level recognition)
* The presence of a profile is calculated from the credits all his original edits have received.
* Presence is assigned to profiles(including originals) not works, because it is a measure of the artist’s overall impact, not just individual pieces.

👉 Recognition becomes:
not volume-based, but meaning-based

🎨 10. Content Layer
FrameHouse supports:
* Edits (video)
* Posters (image)
* Theories (text)

Card System
All content is mapped into 4 containers:
* IMAX (cinematic hero)
* WIDE (standard edits)
* VERTICAL (mobile edits)
* SQUARE (Theories/posters)

👉 No distortion👉 No padding👉 Fully immersive

🧱 11. Theatre (The Core Experience)
Concept
A 2D infinite cinematic canvas

* multidirectional exploration
* spatial navigation
* cluster-based layout

Grid System
* 12-column structure
* 9-row fixed clusters

Clusters
Each cluster:
* fixed size
* contains multiple cards
* tiles perfectly with others

👉 Result:
infinite, seamless canvas with no gaps

🧠 12. Layout Engine
FrameHouse uses:
cluster-based deterministic layout

Rules:
* max 2 edits per cluster
* at least 1 script (breathing space)
* posters as medium-weight elements

Goal:
visual rhythm, not density

🌌 13. Interaction Model

entering a space, not scrolling content
